Bread of Demeter

Description

Demeter, the goddess of agriculture, is closely associated with bread and the harvest. In her honor, bread was a sacred food.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  • Mix flour, salt, and water until a dough forms. Knead and bake at 375°F for 25 minutes. Enjoy with olive oil as Demeter’s devotees might have done.
