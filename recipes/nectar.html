Nectar

Description

Nectar was the drink consumed by the gods, often paired with ambrosia. This elixir was said to maintain the gods' immortality and vitality.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pomegranate juice
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Fresh mint leaves

Instructions

  • Stir together pomegranate juice, orange juice, and honey. Serve chilled, garnished with fresh mint for a refreshing divine drink.
