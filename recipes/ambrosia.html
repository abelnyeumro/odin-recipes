Ambrosia

Description

Ambrosia was considered the food that granted immortality and was served on Mount Olympus by the gods. It's often described as a divine substance with a sweet and fragrant taste, likely akin to honey or nectar.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups honey
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 cup figs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup almonds, toasted

Instructions

  • Mix Greek yogurt with honey and fold in the figs and pomegranate seeds. Top with toasted almonds for a godly dessert.
